Recognizable Gestures

Next Slide

Moves to the next slide.

Previous Slide

Moves to the previous slide.

Pause / Resume

Freezes gesture detection on the current slide. Use the same gesture to resume.

Zoom In

Zooms in on the active slide (only in pause mode).

Zoom Out

Zooms out from the slide (only in pause mode).
